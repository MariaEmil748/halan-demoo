Our Founders

Halan was founded in 2017 by Mounir Nakhla, Founder and CEO, and Ahmed Mohsen, Co-Founder and Chief Technology Officer, both serial entrepreneurs. Nakhla’s impressive track record includes launching two successful companies, Mashroey and Tasaheel, while Mohsen’s extensive experience spans founding multiple technology companies and expertise in computer science, mathematics, computer security, artificial intelligence, and FinTech.

Driven by their entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to social impact, Nakhla and Mohsen have turned their shared vision into a thriving enterprise that upholds excellence at every level.

Our continued success is built on a vibrant startup culture that fosters determination, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to achieving the founders’ vision.
